# AM Brief — test01

## Problem
People eating out often miscalculate tips or skip tipping because math is awkward in the moment. A quick tool on their phone removes the mental load.

## Target User
Anyone at a restaurant, bar, or cafe who wants to know exactly what to leave — no account needed, no learning curve.

## Success Criteria
- User enters a bill amount
- User picks or types a tip percentage (15%, 18%, 20%, custom)
- App shows tip amount and final total instantly
- Works on mobile and desktop
- No backend, no reload needed

## Scope
- Single HTML file (index.html)
- Two inputs: bill amount, tip %
- Two outputs: tip amount, total
- 3 preset tip buttons (15%, 18%, 20%) + custom input
- Plain language labels ("Your tip", "Total to pay")
- No external dependencies, no backend

## Timeline
- Build: ~30 minutes
- Review: ~15 minutes
- Total: under 1 hour

## Out of Scope
- Split bill among multiple people
- Receipt saving
- Dark mode
- Any backend or data storage
